Objective:To explore the efficacy and safety of electric acupuncture combined with aripiprazole in treating negative symptoms of schizophrenia.Methods:60 patients with negative symptoms of schizophrenia who were hospitalized in Nanping Psychiatry Hospital between January 2005 and December 2006,they were randomly assigned to two groups,one group was treated by electric acupuncture combined with aripiprazole(research group) n=30,the other group was treated by the simple aripiprazole(control group)n=30,for 12 weeks.Effects and side effects were assessed with the Positive and Negative Symptom Scale(PANSS) and Treatment Emergent Symptom Scale(TESS) before and after respectively 2、4、8、12 weeks treatment.Results: The efficacy of research group is better than the control group(P0.05).And the research group took effect more early time than the control group(P0.05).But there was no different in side effect between the two groups(P0.05).Conclusions: Electric acupuncture combined with aripiprazole in treatment of negative symptoms of schizophrenia has faster efficacy and better safety.
